Why?

The reason this page exists is secondarily because I want to put more content on my website and because not seeing the ratings of a book are going to make my thoughts on it more impartial, and primarily because I can't trust aggregate review sites not to delete my stuff. This was specifically prompted by the removal of a sarcastic review from MyAnimeList for "not following content guidelines." Luckily I was extended the courtesy of having my review actually sent to me and having an explanation provided (even if I disagreed with the provided explanation), so I was able to put it on my site.

I've heard similar horror stories from Goodreads, where many controversial reviews were taken down, and in this case the content of the reviews were not preserved. While I'm sure that the people at aggregate review sites like MyAnimeList and Goodreads are wonderful people and I appreciate the services that they provide, such centralized authorities and their regulations would only serve to inhibit my content, even though their policies may make their site overall a better place. Therefore I have decided that my content will exist on my own page.
